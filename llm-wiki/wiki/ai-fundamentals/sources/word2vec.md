## Summary

Mikolov et al. introduce **Word2Vec**—efficient methods for learning high-quality word embeddings from large corpora. Word2Vec demonstrated that simple neural network architectures could capture semantic and syntactic word relationships.

## Key Claims

- **Skip-gram and CBOW**: Two efficient training architectures
- **Semantic relationships**: "king - man + woman ≈ queen"
- **Sub-sampling**: Efficient handling of frequent words
- **Foundation for modern NLP embeddings**: Precursor to contextual embeddings
