## Summary

Goodfellow et al. introduce **GANs**—a generative model framework where two neural networks (generator and discriminator) compete in a minimax game. This adversarial training produces high-quality synthetic samples and launched the generative AI era.

## Key Claims

- **Adversarial training**: Generator vs. Discriminator minimax game
- **High-quality samples**: Competitive with other generative methods
- **Foundation for modern generative AI**: StyleGAN, Stable Diffusion, etc.
