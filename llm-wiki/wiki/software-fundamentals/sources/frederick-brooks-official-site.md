## Summary

Frederick P. Brooks, Jr. 是北卡罗来纳大学教堂山分校 Kenan Professor，图灵奖得主（1999），软件工程奠基人。其主页完整列出了职业生涯代表性出版物（书籍 4 本、论文数十篇）及主要荣誉奖项。他的研究涵盖计算机架构、虚拟现实、软件工程和设计科学。

## Key Publications

- **The Design of Design: Essays from a Computer Scientist** (Addison-Wesley, 2010, 400 pages)
- **Computer Architecture: Concepts and Evolution** (with Blaauw, Addison-Wesley, 1997, 1213 pages)
- **The Mythical Man-Month: Essays on Software Engineering, 20th Anniversary Edition** (Addison-Wesley, 1995, 322 pages)
- **Automatic Data Processing, System/360 Edition** (with Iverson, Wiley, 1963)

## Major Honors

- A.M. Turing Award, ACM (1999)
- National Medal of Technology (1985)
- Bower Award and Prize in Science, Franklin Institute (1995)
- Eckert-Mauchly Award, ACM/IEEE (2004)
- Allen Newell Award, ACM (1994)
