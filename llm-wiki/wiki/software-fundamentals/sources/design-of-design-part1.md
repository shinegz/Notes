## Summary

Kenneth M. Anderson 基于 Fred Brooks 的《设计的设计》前三章整理的课程讲义，系统讲解 Brooks 的核心设计问题：工程师如何看待设计（理性模型）、该模型的局限、以及 Brooks 对设计本质的思考。强调设计既需要系统性方法也需要直觉创新。

## Key Claims

- **设计问题（The Design Question）** — 设计的核心问题是如何平衡理性分析与直觉创造。
- **理性设计模型的局限** — 纯理性的设计模型无法处理情感、审美和文化因素。
- **Brooks 的写作风格** — 充满思辨性警句，需要仔细阅读才能体会深意。例如："Systematic designs excluding intuition yields pedestrian follow-ons and knock-offs; intuitive design without system yields flawed fancies."

## Key Quotes

> "Systematic designs excluding intuition yields pedestrian follow-ons and knock-offs; intuitive design without system yields flawed fancies." — Fred Brooks, *The Design of Design*

> "Partitioning a design task is itself an added task. The crisp and precise definition of the interfaces between subtasks is a lot of work, slighted at peril." — Fred Brooks
