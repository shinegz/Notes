## Concepts

- [[sdd/concepts/Spec-First|Spec-First]] — 先写规范再编码，规范可丢弃（最轻量层级）
- [[sdd/concepts/Spec-Anchored|Spec-Anchored]] — 规范作为长期锚点，随功能演进
- [[sdd/concepts/Spec-as-Source|Spec-as-Source]] — 规范即唯一源文件，代码由 AI 生成
- [[sdd/concepts/Agentic-Skills|Agentic Skills]] — 可组合、自动激活的技能框架
- [[sdd/concepts/Compounding-Knowledge|Compounding Knowledge]] — 知识复利，每次迭代让后续更容易
